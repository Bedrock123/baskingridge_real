Our History

Basking Ridge Country Club Catering is open to the community and accessible for all types of events. Only 45 minutes from New York City and 30 minutes from Newark Airport, the Basking Ridge Country Club has the perfect blend of suburban tranquility and metropolitan sophistication. Our facility is contemporary & elegant, featuring raised hearth fireplaces, mirrored French doors, crystal chandeliers and customizable lighting. Newly renovated and under Delicious Heights management, let our unsurpassed service cater to your every need.
